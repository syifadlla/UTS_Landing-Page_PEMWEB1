About Us


WE ARE BOLD, FOCUS & SPIRITED We believe in our strength, genuine creativity, and winning mentality. We believe in our vision to be the best, determined to conquer every challenge. We believe in the passion of the game reflected in sweat and tears to win the game.

WE EMPOWER PEOPLE
Giving them all the power to push beyond their limitations and make their dream come true. We provide extensive design with the latest technology and innovation in every product. Our purpose is to be part of your step in pursuing your dream and loyally accompany you to train, perform and excel on the field.

THE SYMBOL OF OUR PERSONALITY Inspired by the essence of sunrise, we intend to draw the spirit and struggle to rise, “Ortus” which means sunrise in Latin. Visually embodied with a spark rising up, which symbolizes growth, focus, and hope. Our logo explains how we will always learn and evolve. That spirit drives us to support our athletes further and empower our audience’s lifestyles
